Minifigs released each year

This graphs displays the number of LEGO minifigures that appeared for the first time in each year. A minifigure/minifig is a small plastic articulated figurine that usually consists of several parts, often including a head, torso, legs, and hair, hats, and other accessories. Watch how more and more unique minifigs are introduced, or click on the graph and look to the right to see what minifigs were introduced that year.
