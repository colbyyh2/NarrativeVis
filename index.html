History of LEGO

A Narrative Visualization

The LEGO Group is a Danish company founded in 1932. The company has been producing toys consisting of sets of interlocking brick-shaped plastic parts since 1947. The following slideshow explores several aspects of the history of these sets, including the different themes of the sets, their sizes, and LEGO figurines or minifigs in these sets.

Data is sourced from the Rebrickable database.
